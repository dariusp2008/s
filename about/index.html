About NicheChems

Certified reference materials, straightforward crypto checkout.

Who we are, and a plain-language walkthrough of buying Ethereum and paying with a wallet, since that's the only way we currently accept payment.

NicheChems supplies research chemicals, isotope-labeled internal standards, calibration buffers, high-purity solvents, and forensic reference panels to verified research professionals worldwide. Every lot ships with a Certificate of Analysis and Safety Data Sheet, and every product on the site is managed through our internal catalogue tools, so what you see here reflects current stock and pricing, not a stale spec sheet.

We run a lean, research-focused operation. To keep overhead down, and pass that through in pricing, we accept payment in Ethereum (ETH) only: no card processors, no invoicing delays. If you've never paid with crypto before, the guide below covers everything you need: buying ETH, moving it to a wallet you control, and connecting that wallet at checkout.

Paying With Ethereum

A guide to buying ETH and connecting a wallet

Four steps, start to finish. Budget 15–20 minutes if this is your first time, most of that is exchange identity verification, not the crypto part.

  1. Buy ETH on an exchange

    Sign up with a reputable exchange. Coinbase, Kraken, and Binance are the largest and all support bank transfers or card purchases. Complete their identity verification (required by law), then buy the ETH amount you need.

    Check your order total in USD at checkout first, since ETH's price moves. Buy a little extra to cover network fees and price movement between purchase and payment.

  2. Move it to a wallet you control

    An exchange account isn't a wallet, you don't hold the private keys. Install a self-custody wallet before you pay us:

    • MetaMask (browser extension + mobile)
    • Rainbow (mobile)
    • Trust Wallet (mobile)

    Set it up, securely back up the seed phrase it gives you, then withdraw your ETH from the exchange to your new wallet.

  3. Connect your wallet at checkout

    Add products to your cart and proceed to Checkout. On the payment page, click Connect Wallet. Your wallet extension or app opens a popup:

    • Pick the account you want to pay from, then approve the connection
    • Check the network shown is Ethereum Mainnet, switch it in your wallet if it shows anything else
    • Confirm the ETH amount in the popup matches your order total
    • Approve / sign the transaction, your wallet will prompt you once more before anything sends

  4. Confirm your payment

    After sending, copy the transaction hash from your wallet's activity log and email it to us along with your order reference (both are on the payment page) so we can confirm it on-chain and fulfill your order.
